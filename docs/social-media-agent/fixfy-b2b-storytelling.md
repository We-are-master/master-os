# Fixfy B2B — Storytelling Framework

> Reference brain for the Social Media Designer agent (blog + social) and all Fixfy B2B brand copy.
> Market: UK. Currency: £. Spelling: UK. Tone: close & human.

## 1. The core truth (insight)
Everyone who looks after a space — whether they manage 200 flats, run a café, or own one
rental — carries the same quiet stress: when something breaks, it becomes *their* problem.
Finding someone reliable, chasing quotes, hoping they show up, hoping they don't overcharge.

**Maintenance is the second job nobody signed up for.**

## 2. The villain
Not "bad tradespeople" — it's the **chaos and mental load**: the "let me find someone"
spiral, the WhatsApp graveyard of half-finished jobs, the cowboy-builder anxiety. Fixfy
fights the *weight of having to sort everything yourself*, not people.

## 3. The transformation (arc)
**From** "I have to deal with this" → **to** "it's sorted."
The busy person is the hero. Fixfy is the **right-hand** that takes maintenance off their
plate: they forward the problem, we bring the vetted pro, the clear price, and proof it's done.

## 4. StoryBrand (customer = hero, Fixfy = guide)
- **Hero:** the person responsible for keeping a place running.
- **Problem:** external — things break · internal — stress + no time · philosophical — you
  deserve to focus on your real work.
- **Guide:** Fixfy — empathy ("maintenance shouldn't eat your week") + authority (vetted UK
  pros, transparent £, proof).
- **Plan:** 1) Tell us what you need · 2) We match the right pro · 3) It's sorted, with proof.
- **Call to action:** "Forward it to Fixfy."
- **Success:** maintenance off your plate, everything running, tenants/customers happy.
- **Failure avoided:** no cowboy builders, no chasing, no surprise bills.

## 5. Positioning
> For anyone responsible for keeping a property running in the UK, Fixfy is the maintenance
> right-hand that takes the whole job off your plate — vetted local pros, clear prices, and
> proof it's done — so you can get back to what matters.

**Category:** *your outsourced maintenance department.* Master line: **"Maintenance, handled."**

## 6. Messaging pillars (with reasons to believe)
1. **Handed over, not handled by you** — you delegate, you don't execute. *"Just forward it to Fixfy."*
2. **People you can trust** — vetted, insured UK pros (anti-cowboy).
3. **No surprises** — clear £ quotes, proof on completion.
4. **One place for everything** — every trade, every property, one partner.

## 7. Tone of voice (close & human)
Sounds like a helpful colleague, not a corporate vendor. Short sentences. Plain UK English.
"You" and "we". Calm, reassuring, dry British wit — never cheesy. **Name the stress, then relieve it.**

- ✅ Our words: *sorted, handled, right-hand, no fuss, on it, peace of mind, one less thing.*
- 🚫 Avoid: *cheap, revolutionary, disrupt, synergy, solutions provider,* jargon, hype.

Voice example:
- ❌ "Fixfy is a revolutionary platform delivering best-in-class maintenance solutions."
- ✅ "Something broke? Forward it to us. We'll bring someone you can trust — and tell you the price before they start."

- We'll take it from here.

## 9. Blog strategy (3×/week)
Three rotating pillars — one per weekly slot:

**A · Sorted** (practical / how-to) — maintenance guidance for property people. SEO workhorse.
- "Landlord gas safety: what you actually need to know"
- "The 5 repairs every office puts off (and what they cost later)"
- "How to spot a job that needs a pro, not a patch"

**B · Trusted** (trust / industry) — vetting, compliance, anti-cowboy, transparency. Authority.
- "How to tell a vetted tradesperson from a chancer"
- "Insured, certified, checked: what 'vetted' should actually mean"
- "Why the cheapest quote usually costs the most"

**C · Off your plate** (stories / brand) — the human cost of chaos, delegation, real cases.
- "The hidden hours you lose chasing tradespeople"
- "How a property manager got their evenings back"
- "What a maintenance right-hand actually does for you"

Each post: human opening that names the reader's pain → practical value → soft CTA ("forward it to Fixfy").

## 10. Social storytelling (weekly series)
- **Sorted Stories** — a real problem → handled (before/after)
- **Trust signals** — vetted / insured / compliance
- **One less thing** — relief/benefit one-liners
- **Ask Fixfy** — quick maintenance tip
- **Cowboy-buster** — industry myth-busting
