# Fixfy Trade — Partner Guide

**Who this is for:** Tradespeople who work with Fixfy — plumbers, electricians, builders, and other service partners. No technical background needed.

**What this covers:** How to apply, sign in, use the Fixfy Partner app, respond to leads and job invitations, complete work, upload documents, and get paid.

---

## 1. What is Fixfy Trade?

Fixfy Trade is Fixfy's partner network. As a partner you:

- Receive **job invitations** matched to your skills and area
- **Submit prices** when Fixfy is quoting work for a customer
- **Do the job** on site — with schedule, navigation, and time tracking in the app
- **Submit reports** with photos and notes
- **Get paid** on a clear schedule — Fixfy invoices the customer; you receive a self-bill payout

You do not chase clients for money. Fixfy handles the customer relationship and pays you.

```mermaid
flowchart LR
  Invite[Invitation or Lead] --> Bid[Submit bid or Accept job]
  Bid --> Work[Travel and complete work]
  Work --> Report[Submit reports]
  Report --> Pay[Weekly self-bill and payout]
```

### Two places you will use

| Place | What it is |
|-------|------------|
| **Fixfy Trade website** | Sign in page and application form (in your browser) |
| **Fixfy Partner app** | Your day-to-day app on your phone — jobs, schedule, earnings, documents |

The website is the front door. The app is where you run your Fixfy work.

## 2. Getting started

### Step 1 — Apply to become a partner

Go to the **Become a Partner** page and complete the three steps:

| Step | What you provide |
|------|------------------|
| **Account** | Your name, email, phone, and password |
| **Business** | Company name, address, trades you do, tax reference (UTR if self-employed) |
| **Documents** | Required files — see section 6 below |

After you submit:

- Your status is **Onboarding**
- The Fixfy team reviews your application and documents
- You will be notified when you are approved and can start receiving work

> **All documents are required** to process your application. Make sure photos are clear and files are readable.

### Step 2 — Sign in

Once approved, go to the **Fixfy Trade sign-in page**:

1. Enter your **work email**
2. You receive a **6-digit code** by email
3. Enter the code
4. You are taken to the **Fixfy Partner app**

**Sign in** is for partners who already have an account.  
**Create account** on the same page is for approved partners who need to set up login for the first time.

If you see "This email is not linked to a Fixfy Trade partner account", you may need to apply first or contact support.

**Need help?** Email support@getfixfy.com

### Step 3 — Download the app

After your first sign-in, use the **Fixfy Partner** app on your phone for daily work. It is available on the App Store (search for Fixfy Partner / Master Services).

Keep the app installed and notifications on so you do not miss invitations.

## 3. The Fixfy Partner app — what each area is for

When you open the app, think of it in five areas:

### Job invitations

**What it is:** Notifications when Fixfy has work for you.

**When you get one:**

- A **quote is in Bidding** — Fixfy wants your price before sending a proposal to the customer
- A **job is assigned to you** — the customer has already agreed; you need to accept and attend

**What to do:** Open the invitation, read the details (location, type of work, timing), and respond quickly. Fast responses help you win more work.

### My jobs and schedule

**What it is:** All jobs currently assigned to you — dates, addresses, and status.

**What you can do:**

- See when and where you need to be
- Open navigation to the property
- View job details — type of work, customer notes, photos from the office

### Leads

**What it is:** Early opportunities the Fixfy team publishes before they have a full quote.

**Who sees them:** Only partners whose **trade** and **coverage area** match, and who have opted in to receive leads.

**What to do:** If you are interested, tap **Contact**. The Fixfy team sees your name and can involve you when they build the quote.

> A lead is not a confirmed job. It is an expression of interest.

### Submitting bids (quoting)

When a quote is in **Bidding**, you may be invited to submit your price.

**What to include:**

- Your **labour price** for the work described
- **Notes** — access issues, assumptions, extra visits needed
- **Materials** if you are supplying them

The Fixfy team compares bids and builds the customer proposal. You are not sending the quote directly to the customer — Fixfy does that.

### On-site workflow — doing the job

This is the core of your day:

| Step | What you do |
|------|-------------|
| **1. Accept** | Confirm you will attend at the scheduled time |
| **2. Travel** | Use in-app navigation to the property |
| **3. Start** | Tap to start the job when you arrive — this starts the work timer |
| **4. Work** | Complete the agreed scope; take photos as you go |
| **5. Reports** | Submit a **start report** (if required) and a **final report** when finished |
| **6. Complete** | Mark the job complete in the app |

The Fixfy office reviews your reports before invoicing the customer and processing your payment.

### Earnings and payments

**What it is:** A record of what Fixfy owes you for completed work.

**How it works (simple version):**

1. You complete a job and the office approves your reports
2. Your work is added to a **weekly self-bill** — one summary per week
3. Fixfy pays on a **regular schedule** (typically every two weeks on a Friday)
4. You can see payment history and download your self-bill documents in the app

You do not invoice the customer. Fixfy does. You receive payment from Fixfy.

### Profile and coverage

**What it is:** Your business details and where you are willing to work.

**Keep these up to date:**

- Company name and contact name
- Phone and email
- **Coverage area** — either a radius from your base (e.g. 15 miles from SW11) or specific postcode districts
- **Trades** — the types of work you want to receive

If your coverage is too narrow, you will see fewer invitations. If it is too wide, you may get jobs too far away.

### Documents

**What it is:** Your compliance files — insurance, ID, trade certificates.

**Why it matters:** Fixfy must verify you are qualified, insured, and legally allowed to work. Missing or expired documents:

- Lower your **compliance score**
- Can move your account to **Needs Attention**
- May stop you receiving new invitations until fixed

You can upload documents in the app, or via a **document upload link** the Fixfy team sends you by email.

## 4. Your account statuses — what they mean

| Status | What it means for you |
|--------|----------------------|
| **Onboarding** | Application in review — you cannot receive work yet |
| **Active** | Approved — you receive invitations and can bid |
| **Needs Attention** | Something needs fixing — usually expired or missing documents |
| **Inactive** | Not currently receiving work — contact Fixfy if you want to return |

Only **Active** partners receive job invitations and bidding requests.

## 5. Job statuses — what you will see

| Status | What it means |
|--------|----------------|
| **Invitation / Bidding** | Fixfy wants your price — respond with a bid |
| **Scheduled** | You are assigned — attend at the agreed time |
| **In Progress** | You have started work on site |
| **Final Checks** | You submitted reports — office is reviewing |
| **Awaiting Payment** | Approved — waiting for customer payment |
| **Completed** | Job closed — your payment will follow the normal payout schedule |
| **Cancelled** | Job will not go ahead |

## 6. Documents you need

### Everyone must provide

| Document | Why |
|----------|-----|
| **Photo ID** | Proof of identity (passport or driving licence) |
| **Proof of Address** | Utility bill or bank statement |
| **Right to Work** | Legal permission to work in the UK |
| **Public Liability Insurance** | Covers damage or injury during work |

### Self-employed partners

| Document | Why |
|----------|-----|
| **UTR (HMRC)** | Your Unique Taxpayer Reference |

### Limited company partners

| Detail | Why |
|--------|-----|
| **Company Registration Number (CRN)** | Identifies your company |
| **VAT number** | If your company is VAT registered |

### Trade-specific certificates

Depending on your trade, you may also need certificates such as:

| Trade | Examples |
|-------|----------|
| Plumber | Water Regulations, WRAS |
| Electrician | NICEIC, ECS Card, 18th Edition |
| Gas work | Gas Safe Certificate |
| PAT Testing | PAT Testing Certificate |
| EICR | EICR Qualification |
| Fire safety trades | Relevant fire safety certification |

The app and Fixfy team will tell you exactly which certificates apply to your trades.

### Keeping documents valid

- Insurance and certificates have **expiry dates** — renew before they lapse
- Upload the new file as soon as you have it
- Expired documents reduce your compliance score and can pause new work

## 7. Rules and expectations

### Respond quickly

- **Bids:** The faster you submit a price, the more likely you are to be selected
- **Leads:** Tap Contact if you are interested — first responders are often preferred
- **Job invitations:** Accept or decline promptly so the office can plan

### Keep the app active

- Your location helps Fixfy dispatch work efficiently
- If the app has not heard from you for 15+ minutes, you may show as **Offline** on the ops map — this does not remove you from the network, but being **Available** helps you get nearby jobs

### Cancellations

- If you need to cancel a job, do it as early as possible through the app
- Cancelling close to the scheduled start time (within 24 hours) may result in a **cancellation fee** — the amount is set by Fixfy and shown in your terms

### Quality and reports

- Take clear photos of work completed
- Fill in report notes accurately — the customer and Fixfy rely on them
- Incomplete reports delay your payment

### Coverage and trades

- Only opt into trades you are qualified to do
- Set a realistic coverage area — you are expected to attend jobs you accept

## 8. Common tasks — step by step

### I received a bidding invitation

1. Open the invitation in the app
2. Read the scope, location, and timing
3. Enter your price and any notes
4. Submit — the Fixfy team will review all bids

### I was assigned a job

1. Confirm the date and time in your calendar
2. On the day, navigate to the property
3. Start the job when you arrive
4. Complete the work and take photos
5. Submit your final report
6. Mark complete

### I need to upload a document

**Option A — in the app:** Go to Documents and upload the file.

**Option B — upload link:** If Fixfy sent you an email with a link, open it on your phone or computer, sign in with the link, and upload the requested file. No full app login needed.

### I am not receiving any work

Check these in order:

1. Is your status **Active**? (Not Onboarding or Inactive)
2. Are your **documents** valid and not expired?
3. Is your **coverage area** set correctly?
4. Have you opted in to **receive leads** (if you want lead notifications)?
5. Are your **trades** selected correctly?
6. Is the app installed with **notifications enabled**?

If all of the above look correct, contact support@getfixfy.com

### When will I get paid?

- Payment follows completed and **office-approved** jobs
- Work is grouped into **weekly self-bills**
- Payouts are on a regular cycle (typically **every two weeks on a Friday**)
- Exact dates appear in the Earnings section of the app

## 11. Frequently asked questions

**Is Fixfy my employer?**  
No. You work as an independent trade partner. Fixfy connects you with customers and handles billing.

**Do I quote the customer directly?**  
No. You submit bids to Fixfy. Fixfy builds and sends the proposal to the customer.

**Can I see all jobs in the country?**  
No. You only see invitations and leads that match your trades, coverage, and account status.

**What if I miss an invitation?**  
Respond as soon as you can. Late responses may mean the job goes to another partner.

**What is a self-bill?**  
A summary of work you completed in a given week — it is the basis for your payout.

**Why was I moved to Needs Attention?**  
Usually expired insurance or a missing certificate. Upload the renewed document to restore your score.

**Can I work while Onboarding?**  
No. Wait until Fixfy activates your account.

**I forgot my sign-in email.**  
Contact support@getfixfy.com with your company name and phone number.
