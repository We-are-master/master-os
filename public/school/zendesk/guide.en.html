Part 1 — The Fixfy system

Welcome

Fixfy connects B2B customers (Housekeep, Checkatrade and other marketplaces) with tradespeople (electricians, plumbers, painters, etc.) to resolve property maintenance. You, the agent, are the bridge — making sure the request flows correctly from customer to partner and finishes well.

One-sentence summary

Customer requests something, the ticket lands in Zendesk, you triage, apply a macro, and the OS (central system at app.getfixfy.com) takes it from there.

You and your team

Fixfy Zendesk has 3 main agents:

Carlos Perez - Pre-sale / Triage

  • Recebe TODOS os tickets novos (status New, Open)
  • Decide: e um Job, Quote, Complaint, ou Finance?
  • Acompanha Quotes em Bidding ate aprovacao
  • Hands to Isabela when the ticket moves to execution

Isabela Gil - Execution / Operations

  • Receives tickets in execution (Unassigned, Schedule, In Progress, Final Checks)
  • Acompanha o partner do inicio ao fim
  • Handles Complaints (with partner investigation)
  • Resolve when job is complete and send report to the customer

Victor Braz - Finance / Admin

  • Recebe SO tickets em status Finance
  • Refunds, disputas, cobrancas duplicadas
  • Has private Action Required (All) view for supervision

Part 2 — The 9 Views

Views are automatic filters. Each View shows only tickets that match its conditions. You don't "move" tickets — they can appear in multiple views at the same time.

Customer Support :: Action Required

Role: your main queue. Tickets that need your action now.

Enters: new ticket, customer replied, partner replied, validation failed.

Leaves: you apply a macro (goes to Unassigned/Bidding/etc.), you send a Public Reply (goes to Needs Reply), or apply Solved.

Key filter: includes On Hold ONLY if reply_status = awaiting. If you already replied, the ticket leaves here and goes to Needs Attention.

Customer Support :: Needs Attention

Role: open Complaints + On Hold tickets (any state).

Why: you don't need to ACT but you need to MONITOR. Check twice a day.

Customer Support :: Quoting

Role: active Quotes (Ready to Quote, Bidding, Approval). Check 3 times a day.

Customer Support :: Jobs

Role: Jobs in execution (Unassigned, Schedule, In Progress, Final Checks).

Partner Support :: Inquiries

Role: partner tickets (onboarding, disputes, support).

Finance :: Finance Support

Role: tickets in Finance status. Only Victor works this queue.

WhatsApp, Solved

WhatsApp via OApps + closed tickets.

Part 5 — The 6 macros

You have ONLY 6 macros. Deliberately simple — fewer options, fewer mistakes.

How to apply a macro

1 Open the ticket
2 Below the reply field, find "Apply macro"
3 Start typing the name (e.g. "job") — suggestions appear
4 Select it
5 IMPORTANT: click Submit to save and trigger
Macro does NOT apply by itself

Selecting the macro only PROPOSES changes. You must click Submit for them to take effect. If you leave without Submit, you lose everything. This is the most common beginner mistake.

1. Move to Job

When: customer wants to book a service (date + price)

  • Form: Job
  • Status: Unassigned (ou Auto Assigning)
  • Tag: job-created
  • Webhook → OS creates job

2. Move to Quote

When: customer wants a quote first

  • Form: Quote
  • Status: Ready to Quote
  • Tag: sent-to-os
  • Bid: side conversations auto

3. Move to Complaint

When: customer complains about completed work

  • Form: Complaint
  • Status: 🚨 Complaint
  • Tag: in_complaint
  • Assigns Isabela

4. Move to Finance

When: refund, dispute, billing

  • Form: Finance
  • Status: 🟣 Finance
  • Tag: finance_support
  • Assigns Victor

5. Mark as Cancelled

When: cancelling the ticket

  • Form: Cancelled
  • Status: ❌ Cancelled
  • Tag: cancelled
  • REQUIRED: Cancellation Reason

6. Mark as Solved

When: work complete, close ticket

  • Status: ✅ Solved
  • Clears pending tags
  • Does not switch form

Part 7 — Detailed workflows

Receive new ticket — triage

1 Open Action Required
2 Take the top ticket (oldest)
3 Read the message carefully
4 Ask: is it Job, Quote, Complaint, Finance, Cancelled or already Solved?
5 If unsure: do NOT apply a macro — ask the team

Create Job (step by step)

Checklist before the macro

  • Form: Job (or not needed — macro switches it)
  • Client Name filled in
  • Client Email filled in
  • Client Phone
  • Address (street/place) + UK postcode (in the same field or separate postcode field — e.g. 124 City Road + EC1V 2NX)
  • Type of Work
  • Job Date
  • Arrival Time
  • Rate Type: Hourly ou Fixed
  • Se Fixed: Client Price
  • Scope
  • Auto-Assign: True ou False

What happens next

  • Zendesk fires webhook to OS with payload
  • OS creates job, responds with job_id (e.g. JB-2026-1234)
  • If Auto-Assign=True and partners match: status becomes 🤖 Auto Assigning, OS sends invites
  • If Auto-Assign=True but no partner matches (trade, postcode, slot): 🔴 Unassigned — assign manually or fix address/postcode
  • If Auto-Assign=False: becomes 🔴 Unassigned, you assign manually
  • Rate Type blank on smart-price services (Gardener, Plumber…): OS infers hourly with min 2h billed — still fill Rate Type on the ticket
  • Ticket leaves Action Required — goes to Isabela

Criar Quote (Bid vs Manual)

Bid

You don't know the price. You want partners to send competing proposals.

Manual

You already know the standard price (e.g. GSC costs £80). You set it yourself.

Steps

1 Preencha: Client Name, Email, Address, TOW, Scope (detalhado!)
2 Quote Mode: Bid ou Manual
3 Apply macro: Move to Quote
4 Submit

Abrir Complaint

When to use

  • Customer complains about service quality
  • Partner didn't show / arrived late
  • Cobranca incorreta

When NOT to use

  • Customer only asks for status (reply in Job form)
  • Customer wants to rebook (use Job)

Steps

1 Open the ticket
2 Read the complaint twice
3 Write Public Reply: "Received, we'll investigate and get back within 24h"
4 Apply macro: Move to Complaint
5 Submit
6 Fill in: On Hold Reason (1 of 14), Complaint Description, Solution (later)

Cancel

1 Open the ticket
2 Apply macro: Mark as Cancelled
3 Submit
4 Form switches to Cancelled — Cancellation Reason field appears
5 Choose ONE of the 7 reasons (REQUIRED)
6 If Other: fill in Cancellation Notes too
7 Save

Available reasons

  • Client Requested Cancellation
  • Customer No Longer Requires Service
  • Duplicate Booking
  • Budget Issues
  • Customer Found Alternative Contractor
  • Delay No Longer Acceptable
  • Other (add details below)

Finance flow

1 Carlos/Isabela sees ticket with financial matter
2 Apply macro: Move to Finance
3 Submit
4 Auto-assigned to Victor
5 Victor resolves in Finance form
6 Mark as Solved when complete

Mark as Solved

Before applying

  • Confirm there are NO pending customer issues
  • Partner finished
  • Customer was informed of the resolution

Steps

1 Apply macro: Mark as Solved
2 Submit

Part 8 — OS sync

How webhooks work

Webhook = automatic message Zendesk sends to the OS when something happens on the ticket. You don't need to do anything — when you apply a macro, the webhook fires automatically.

Sync directions

ActionDirectionWhen it fires
Create JobZendesk → OSMacro Move to Job applied
Create QuoteZendesk → OSMacro Move to Quote
Cancel JobZendesk → OSMacro Mark as Cancelled
Hold JobZendesk → OSTag on_hold aplicada
Update Job statusOS → ZendeskOS updates status (e.g. partner accepted)
Sync ReasonsOS → ZendeskAdmin edita lista no OS

Idempotency

Same ticket = 1 job in OS

If you apply Move to Job 5 times on the same ticket, OS detects by ticket_id and returns the same job (action "existing"). No duplicate created.

Part 9 — Troubleshooting

Common errors

"Job Missing Data" after the macro

Cause: required fields empty.

Fix: fill in the empty fields and apply the macro again.

"cancel_missing_reason" after Mark as Cancelled

Cause: you forgot to choose a Cancellation Reason.

Fix: open the ticket, choose a reason, apply Mark as Cancelled again.

Ticket fica em loop entre 2 statuses

Cause: conflicting trigger.

Fix: call dev. You can't fix this alone.

client_name no OS sai como "Checkatrade" em vez do nome real

Cause: you forgot to fill in Client Name.

Fix: fill in manually; apply macro again to resend.

"No hourly_client_rate available" do OS

Cause: Account has no pricing configured for that Type of Work.

Fix: tell ops to configure pricing in the OS.

Webhook 401 ou 403

Cause: webhook authentication expired.

Fix: admin updates credentials in Admin Center → Webhooks.

When to call dev

  • Webhook keeps failing even with fields filled
  • Infinite status loop
  • OS accepts payload but doesn't show correct data
  • Macro applied but trigger didn't fire
  • Strange tag appearing automatically
  • Pricing missing (OS-side fix needed)

FAQ

Can I apply 2 macros on the same ticket?

Yes, but avoid doing it at once. Apply one, see the result, apply another later if needed.

What if the customer replied before the macro triggered the webhook?

No problem. Webhook fires on Submit. Customer reply doesn't interfere.

Webhook failed. Should I apply the macro again?

Yes if a field was missing. Applying again fires the webhook again.

Can I create a new macro?

No. Admin only. Ask the team.

Where do I see tickets I already solved?

Views > Solved (today) ou busca filtrando por assignee:me status:solved.

Customer asks for status — where do I look?

Open the ticket. For operational details (partner ETA), check the OS.

How do I take an unassigned ticket?

Open → Assignee → Take it. OR auto-routing assigns automatically.

Why are there 2 Quote emails?

Public Reply goes to customer. Side Conversations go to partners. Customer only sees Public Reply.

Do I have to reply outside business hours?

No. Standard hours 9am–5pm weekdays.

How do I know the OS received my webhook?

OS response arrives: check ticket audit or webhook invocation history in Admin Center.
