What the partner network is

Fixfy Trade is Fixfy's partner network. As a partner you receive job invitations, submit prices, execute jobs, deliver reports and get paid — without chasing the customer yourself.

TWO PLACES

Fixfy Trade website — browser login and application. Fixfy Partner app — day-to-day on your phone (jobs, schedule, earnings).

The partner cycle — from opportunity to payment
BenefitWhat it means
Jobs sent to youQualified local work matched to your trade and postcode
Faster paymentFixfy invoices the customer; you get paid on a clear schedule
Less adminSchedule, jobs and earnings in one app

Apply, sign-in and app

Step 1 — Apply

On the Become a Partner page — three steps:

StepWhat to submit
AccountName, email, phone, password
BusinessCompany, address, trades, UTR (if self-employed)
DocumentsID, insurance, proof of address, right to work

After submit: status Onboarding until the Fixfy team activates you.

Step 2 — Sign in

  1. Work email at /trade/login
  2. 6-digit code by email
  3. Opens the Fixfy Partner app

Help: support@getfixfy.com

Step 3 — App on your phone

Install Fixfy Partner (App Store). Keep notifications on so you don't miss invitations.

MODULE 4
TRADE

Contact and pricing

LEADS

Ops publishes opportunities on Trade. You only see them if trade and area match. Contact = I want to be involved. Not a confirmed job.

Bidding (quoting)

Quote in Bidding — invitation to submit a price:

  • Labour price
  • Materials if applicable
  • Notes (access, assumptions)

Fixfy compares bids and sends a proposal to the customer. You do not speak to the customer directly.

Bidding flow

App invite→ Your price→ Fixfy reviews→ Job assigned

Expectations and best practices

  • Respond quickly to bids, leads and invitations
