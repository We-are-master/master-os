COMPLIANCE

Certified Trades Services

For regulated and specialist works, we operate with certified engineers and qualified contractors. Compliance is non-negotiable.

Gas Safe Engineers

Gas Safe is the official gas registration body in the UK. Any engineer working with boilers, gas appliances, pipework or heating systems must legally be Gas Safe registered.

This ensures safety, compliance and insurance protection for the property and everyone in it.

ServiceTypical SKU
Gas Safety Certificate (CP12)(GSC) Annual landlord certificate
Boiler serviceAnnual service and safety check
Boiler repairBreakdown and fault finding

NICEIC & NAPIT Electricians

Electrical compliance is critical for commercial and residential properties. Our certified electricians provide:

  • EICRs — Electrical Installation Condition Reports
  • Fault finding and emergency electrical works
  • Consumer unit upgrades
  • Lighting installation
  • Commercial electrical maintenance
  • PAT testing — Portable Appliance Testing

NICEIC and NAPIT certifications demonstrate that the engineer meets UK safety and competency standards.

OFTEC Engineers

OFTEC certification applies to oil-fired heating systems and specialist heating installations — particularly important for properties using oil heating outside major city centres.

CERTIFICATES IN SERVICES

Certificate SKUs appear under the Certificates category in the Services catalogue — fixed prices, compliance reports uploaded via the Partner app after completion.
